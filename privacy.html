Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Pendula - Chaos in Motion ("we", "our", or "us") is a local physics simulation app.

Data Collection

We do not require account creation and we do not directly collect personal data on our own servers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app may show interstitial ads to free users. AdMob may process device identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads. If a user purchases PRO (Remove Ads), ad display is disabled.

In-App Purchase (Remove Ads PRO)

Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We store only local entitlement state (for example, whether ads should be removed) on-device to preserve purchase behavior between app launches. Purchase records are managed by Google Play and can be restored on eligible devices/accounts.

Device Features

The app can use device orientation, vibration, wake lock, and local audio playback for simulation and feedback. These features operate locally on your device.

Local Storage

The app stores local preferences such as volume, mute state, ad cooldown timestamps, session counters, and premium entitlement flags on your device.

Children

The app is not directed to children under the applicable digital consent age in your region.
